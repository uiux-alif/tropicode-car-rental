Our story

A decade of local expertise on the road

Founded in 2015, Bali Transport started as a small family business with two vehicles and a commitment to giving tourists the best possible experience on the island. Today we operate a fleet of over 20 vehicles serving thousands of travelers every year.

We understand Bali better than anyone — the traffic patterns, the hidden temples, the secret beaches, and the fastest routes during peak season. Our team is made up of passionate local Balinese who take pride in sharing their island with the world.

Every vehicle is personally inspected, cleaned, and maintained before every rental. Whether you need a quick airport transfer or a week-long private driver, we treat every booking as our most important one.
