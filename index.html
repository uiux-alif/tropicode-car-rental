FAQ

Everything you need to know

Common questions before booking with us.

Driver is optional. Choose Self Drive or With Driver when filling the inquiry form. Driver fees are calculated per day and shown in your price estimate before you submit.
Fuel is not included in the base rental price. For self drive, you cover fuel costs. For with driver, fuel is also typically not included unless explicitly agreed.
Self drive requires a valid driver's license (SIM A for Indonesians, international permit for foreigners) and passport or ID. For with driver, passport or ID is sufficient.
